# FESDFA Risk Assessment

## Purpose

This risk assessment identifies potential threats to the FESDFA membership platform and evaluates their likelihood and impact.

## Risk Matrix

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Weak Passwords | Medium | High | High |
| Unauthorized Database Access | Medium | Critical | Critical |
| Data Breach | Low | Critical | High |
| Website Defacement | Low | Medium | Medium |
| Phishing Against Administrators | High | High | High |
| Loss of Website Availability | Medium | High | High |
| QR Code Fraud | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Accidental Data Deletion | Medium | High | High |

## Recommended Mitigation Strategies

### Weak Passwords
- Enforce strong password requirements
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

### Unauthorized Access
- Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Conduct regular access reviews

### Data Breach
- Encrypt sensitive data
- Limit access to confidential information

### Phishing
- Conduct security awareness training
- Verify suspicious requests

### Service Availability
- Maintain backups
- Monitor hosting and database services

## Security Objective

Reduce organizational risk while maintaining secure and reliable access to FESDFA services.
